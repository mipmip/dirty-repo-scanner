## MODIFIED Requirements

### Requirement: Nav bar keybinding display
The nav bar SHALL display all available keybindings on the left side. The keybinding hints SHALL include: q (quit), s (scan), enter (open), tab (switch), jk/arrows (navigate), l (log), pgup/pgdn (scroll), gg/G (jump).

#### Scenario: Nav bar shows all keybindings
- **WHEN** the application is running
- **THEN** the nav bar displays keybinding hints including the new log toggle, scroll, and jump bindings
